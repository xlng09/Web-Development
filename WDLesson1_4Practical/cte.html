Computer Programming and Web Design

Computer Programming & Web Design is a program provides a rigorous academic course of study
which prepares students for post-secondary education and careers in software development.
Students develop a strong foundation in programming concepts in various environments.
Students acquire the confidence to be self learners and explore advance concepts and technologies.
Students observe first hand careers in software development through participation in work
based and career readiness experiences. Students gain valuable workforce skills such as
collaboration, project management and communication.
